Mist-to-Meadow is a project to transform barren coastal lands into thriving green ecosystems by capturing water directly from the air. From the sun-drenched shores of the Mediterranean and the dry coasts of the Sea of Cortez to islands in the Caribbean, vast areas lie beside oceans yet remain arid and lifeless. Not from lack of proximity to water, but from the inability to access it. These barren coastlines contribute to desertification, reduce biodiversity, and miss opportunities for carbon capture.
